Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-07-15

Content OS ("the app") is a personal content-production tool built and operated by a single individual for managing their own social media channels. It is not a public service and does not collect data from, or about, anyone other than its own operator.

What data the app handles

  • OAuth access/refresh tokens for the operator's own connected YouTube, TikTok, and Instagram accounts — used only to publish content to those same accounts, and to read basic account info (e.g. channel/account name) and public performance metrics (views, likes, comments) for videos the app itself published.
  • Video content, titles, descriptions, and related metadata generated by the app.

Where data is stored

All data is stored in a database operated by and accessible only to the app's operator. It is not hosted as a shared/multi-tenant service, and no other individual has an account or access.

Data sharing

Data is not sold, rented, or shared with third parties, except as required to operate the app's core function: publishing content to the platforms (YouTube, TikTok, Instagram) the operator has explicitly connected, via each platform's official API.

Data retention and deletion

The operator can delete any stored video, connected account, or associated data directly within the app at any time. Disconnecting a platform account revokes the app's stored access token for that platform.

Changes

This policy may be updated at any time as the app's functionality changes.

Contact

For questions about this policy, contact the operator via the account used to register this app with the relevant platform's developer program.
